Frequently Asked Questions

What is Faceoff? +
Faceoff is a creative platform where you can compare, vote, and discover art in a fun Renaissance-inspired style.
How do I participate in a Faceoff? +
Simply log in, browse the ongoing faceoffs, and cast your vote for the artwork you like best. You can also submit your own entries!
Is Faceoff free to use? +
Yes! Browsing and voting are free. Some premium features may be introduced in the future, but the core experience will always be accessible.
