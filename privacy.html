1) Information we collect +

We collect information to operate and improve Faceoff:

  • Account data – name/handle, email, password (hashed), profile details.
  • Content – submissions, votes, comments, and related metadata.
  • Usage data – device info, browser type, pages viewed, session analytics, approximate location (derived from IP), and interaction events.
  • Optional data – preferences, notifications settings, and social handles if you choose to link them.
2) How we use your information +
  • Provide core features (hosting faceoffs, voting, rankings, profiles).
  • Maintain safety, integrity, and moderation.
  • Analyze performance and improve the product.
  • Communicate updates, support messages, and service announcements.
  • Personalize content and recommendations where enabled.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.
3) Legal bases (EEA/UK users) +

We rely on: (a) performance of a contract (to provide the service), (b) legitimate interests (e.g., security, analytics), (c) consent where required (e.g., certain cookies/marketing), and (d) compliance with legal obligations.

4) When we share information +
  • Service providers that help us operate (hosting, analytics, email, moderation) under appropriate contracts.
  • Public content you choose to post (e.g., submissions, display name) may be visible to others.
  • Legal & safety – to comply with law, protect rights, or prevent harm.
  • Business transfers – in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.
5) Data retention +

We keep data for as long as needed to provide Faceoff and for legitimate business or legal purposes. Retention periods vary by data type; we delete or anonymize data when it’s no longer required.

6) Security +

We employ administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password and enable available protections.

7) Your rights & choices +
  • Access/Correction/Deletion – request a copy, fix inaccuracies, or ask us to delete where applicable.
  • Portability – receive certain data in a portable format (EEA/UK/other regions where required).
  • Object/Restrict – object to or request restriction of certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent – for processing relying on consent.
  • Marketing – opt out of marketing emails via the link in messages.
  • Do Not Sell/Share (CCPA/CPRA) – California residents may exercise these rights if applicable.

To exercise rights, see Contact us below.

8) Cookies & similar technologies +

We use cookies and similar tech for authentication, preferences, analytics, and performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings; some features may not function correctly if disabled.

9) Children’s privacy +

Faceoff is not directed to children under the age required by local law. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10) International data transfers +

Your information may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

11) Changes to this policy +

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will post updates here and adjust the “Last updated” date.

12) Contact us +

If you have questions or requests regarding privacy, contact us at privacy@faceoff.app.
